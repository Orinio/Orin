# Orin — Product Hunt Launch Copy

## Tagline
**Turn Work Into Career Proof**

## Description (for Product Hunt)

Most developers struggle to prove their skills to employers. Resumes lie. Portfolios are outdated. GitHub profiles are hard to read.

**Orin** fixes this with **Proof Cards** — verified, shareable achievements that showcase your real work.

### How it works:
1. Connect your GitHub, Kaggle, LinkedIn, or certificates
2. Orin's AI agents analyze your work and create Proof Cards
3. Each Proof Card is verified against the source (GitHub API, certificate checksums, etc.)
4. Share your proof profile with recruiters — every skill backed by real proof

### What makes Orin different:
- **8 specialized AI agents** that understand your career (not just a chatbot)
- **Persistent memory** — the AI remembers your goals, skills, and progress
- **Real verification** — not self-reported claims, but cryptographically verified proof
- **Opportunity matching** — AI matches your proof portfolio to jobs, internships, and scholarships
- **Claude-inspired chat** — beautiful streaming interface with visual output

Built with Next.js 16, React 19, Supabase, and NVIDIA NIM (Qwen 3.5 397B).

### One-liner:
**Orin turns your scattered work into verified career proof that recruiters actually trust.**
